========================================
  GEMINI MUSIC GENERATION PROMPT
========================================

Create a 5-minute cyberpunk electronic ambient pop track titled "Interim".

This song is for a space terminal interface (Grace of Touch Terminal) - imagine the aesthetic of a holographic cockpit floating through deep space, with cyan terminal text glowing in the dark.

Style: Cyberpunk ambient pop meets space electronica. Think Blade Runner 2049 soundtrack meets Radiohead's "Kid A" meets Interstellar. Japanese vocals with English phrases.

BPM: 88, Key: Dm (D minor)

Detailed structure:

INTRO (0:00-0:30):
- Sound design: terminal boot sequence, soft keyboard clicks, microphone activation pop
- Slowly building synth pad (warm analog, slightly detuned)
- Sub-bass pulse like a heartbeat
- Atmosphere: floating in space, screens powering on

VERSE 1 (0:30-1:15):
- Whispered Japanese vocals, intimate and close-mic'd
- Minimal piano (Rhodes-like, with subtle tape saturation)
- Pulsing sub-bass (808-style but soft)
- Glitchy vocal artifacts floating in the stereo field
- Light hi-hat pattern, almost imperceptible
- Lyrics theme: uncertain words on a screen, voice in deep space

PRE-CHORUS (1:15-1:35):
- Vocals layer and harmonize
- Arpeggiated synth (analog-style, 16th notes)
- Building tension, filter sweep opening up
- Drums start to emerge (electronic kit, compressed)

CHORUS (1:35-2:15):
- Full electronic drums enter (punchy kick, crisp snare, shimmering cymbals)
- Powerful vocal melody, double-tracked
- Thick synth chord stabs (saw wave + square wave layered)
- The "confirmation" moment - everything becomes clear and bold
- English phrases mixed in: "We are all interim"
- Cymbal crashes on transitions
- Bass drops to root note, powerful and grounding

VERSE 2 (2:15-3:00):
- Return to minimal but with more electronic texture
- Vinyl crackle, voice memo quality processing on some vocal layers
- Add cosmic noise (filtered white noise = cosmic ray sounds)
- Lyrics theme: repeating/correcting yourself, noise contains truth

BRIDGE (3:15-3:50):
- Complete breakdown - voice waveform as sound
- Pitch-shifting vocals (like analyzing a voiceprint)
- Spectral analysis tones (sine waves at specific frequencies)
- Clinical/scientific atmosphere mixed with emotional vulnerability
- Spoken word section: "声紋は嘘をつかない" (voiceprints don't lie)
- Build with countdown: "3... 2... 1..."

FINAL CHORUS (3:50-4:30):
- Everything together, maximum intensity
- Add orchestral synth strings (lush, cinematic)
- Triumphant but melancholic - beautiful sadness
- Extra vocal harmonies, choir-like pads
- Lyrics theme: we are all interim, voices become the future

OUTRO (4:30-5:00):
- Deconstruct back to interim state
- Vocals dissolve into infinite reverb
- Elements drop out one by one
- Final typing sounds growing distant
- Microphone click off
- Silence

Key sonic elements:
- Glitch effects on vocals representing uncertainty (interim state)
- Clean-to-bold transitions representing 薄い→濃い text confirmation
- Terminal/computer sound design throughout
- Voice memo / phone quality vocal layers for intimacy
- Pitch analysis tones in bridge (voiceprint/声紋 reference)
- Keyboard clicking and mouse sounds as subtle percussion
- Space ambience (cosmic noise, distant radio signals)
- Cyan/blue mood in the production (if that makes sense sonically - cold but beautiful)

Mood progression:
Intro: Awakening, alone in space
Verse: Vulnerable, uncertain, searching
Chorus: Clarity, power, confirmation
Bridge: Scientific wonder, self-discovery
Final: Acceptance, hope, launch into the unknown
Outro: Return to beautiful uncertainty

Reference tracks for vibe:
