========================================
  PRODUCTION NOTES
========================================

KEY SONIC ELEMENTS:
- Radio static / frequency scanning as a recurring motif
- Rocket engine rumble as sub-bass texture
- Keyboard typing sounds as percussion layer
- Digital glitch effects on vocals (interim → final transition)
- Voice through noise — use bandpass filter on vocals in verses, open up in chorus
- 3-source motif: play the same melodic phrase 3 ways (clean/distorted/processed) simultaneously in chorus

VOCAL PROCESSING:
- Verse: close-mic, intimate, slight compression
- Pre-chorus: add room reverb, double-track
- Chorus: stadium reverb, harmonies, power
- Bridge: radio communication filter (bandpass 300-3000Hz, add crackle)
- Outro: infinite reverb tail, whispered

GUITAR TONES:
- Verse: clean with slight overdrive, delay
- Chorus: full distortion, wall of sound, two guitars panned L/R
- Bridge: clean arpeggios, tremolo
- Solo: singing lead tone, wah-wah, delay
